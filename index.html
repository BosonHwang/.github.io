About

I am a PhD researcher at Griffith University working on time-series intelligence, multimodal learning, and medical AI. I am supervised by Prof. Shirui Pan and Dr. Ming Jin.

My research focuses on building intelligent systems that understand complex temporal and medical data in a way that is unified, reliable, and interpretable.

Research Interests

  • Time-Series Representation Learning
  • Multimodal and Foundation Models
  • Medical AI and Physiological Signals
  • Clinical and Agentic AI Evaluation
  • Energy Forecasting and Decision-Aware Modeling
